Idea analysis

Eric evaluates and refines ideas to determine their feasibility, relevance, and potential impact on the user experience (user needs, business objectives, feasibility, impact and prioritization).

Concept design

Eric explores and creates high-level design concepts to visualize and communicate design ideas to stakeholders and team members before moving to the more detailed design stages.

High-fidelity implementation

Eric converts design concepts into a fully functional and visually appealing interfaces. He uses design files (Sketch, Figma, or Adobe XD), to create pixel-perfect UI elements, incorporating animations and interactions, and ensuring that the final product meets the design requirements.
